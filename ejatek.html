People with autism spectrum disorder often have difficulties with nonverbal communication, especially understanding facial expressions. These difficulties can lead to misunderstandings and communication barriers during social interactions. Try yourself! How well can you recognize the emotion behind different facial expressions? Identify the correct emotion! Be careful, some emotions can be shown in different ways and might be tricky! It’s important to know that if you click an answer button and don’t move on to the next question, your answer was wrong. If you get a new question, then your answer was correct. Have fun!
